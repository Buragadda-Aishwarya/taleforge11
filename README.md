# TaleForge AI

### AI-Powered Narrative Intelligence Platform

> **Where Ideas Take Shape**

TaleForge AI is a next-generation storytelling platform designed to help writers create, maintain, and expand long-form stories without losing consistency. Unlike traditional AI writing assistants that simply generate text, TaleForge AI combines **long-term memory, continuity intelligence, research capabilities, and multi-agent collaboration** to ensure stories remain coherent, immersive, and engaging.

---

# 🚀 Problem Statement

Writers working on novels, screenplays, fan-fiction, games, and cinematic universes often face:

* Plot holes
* Timeline inconsistencies
* Character drift
* Forgotten story elements
* Re-reading previous chapters
* Creative burnout

As stories grow larger, maintaining narrative consistency becomes increasingly difficult.

### Our Insight

> "The real challenge isn't generating stories — it's remembering them."

# 🏗 Architecture

## Frontend

### React + Tailwind CSS

Provides:

* Story Workspace
* Writing Studio
* Story Bible Dashboard
* Continuity Dashboard
* Research Workspace

---

## Backend

### Node.js + Express

Handles:

* API orchestration
* Story processing
* AI model communication
* Database operations
* Memory retrieval pipelines

---

## Databases

### PostgreSQL

Stores:

* Stories
* Characters
* Relationships
* Locations
* World rules
* Generated outputs

### ChromaDB

Stores:

* Vector embeddings
* Story chunks
* Character facts
* Lore entries
* Research results

Provides long-term semantic memory.

↓

* Characters
* Locations
* Relationships
* World Rules

↓

Stores in PostgreSQL + ChromaDB

↓

Story Bible Dashboard

↓

Retrieve Context from ChromaDB

↓

↓

↓

Provide Explanation + Suggestions
